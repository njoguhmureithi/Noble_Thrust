Menu

The Noble Thrust Foundation is a Human Life Development Entity consisting of various initiatives. It is based on the premise that a fruitful life is the true life. All of life is a school and therefore, Human Life Development is the progressive improvement that takes place in one’s life out of the ordinary and extraordinary day to day exposures and encounters. It is structured based on a facilitator - learner relationship model, to develop people who are effectively and continuously prepared to live and influence based on their knowledge of God, things in their various areas of placement.

POSTED

This is Noble Thrust Foundation’s forum of interaction that aims to develop effective stewardship of life.It speaks to the objective of developing people who see themselves as ambassadors of Christ drawing from Paul’s words to the Corinthians. 2 Corinthians 3:2 You yourselves are our letter, written on our hearts, known, and read by everyone. (NIV)

KEEPer

This is Noble Thrust Foundation’s initiative targeting 12–16-year-olds aiming to have then to be Knowledge Enriched and Engaged Purposefully (K.E.E.P). Biblical based Strategic reading as well as talent development programs are run during the school holidays.Knowledge enrichment is a guided process of joint reading and personal journaling.Purposeful engagement is through structured support to develop various forms of expression according to specific areas of gifting and talents.

STRATEGIC READING

Promotes the use of books as a strategic tool for personal development (positive growth and transformation in all life spheres and dimensions). It involves reading carefully selected healthy personal development books. The goal of reading is to harvest valuable information for application in one’s life, for holistic development.

NOBLE THRUST AFRICA

Noble Thrust Africa is a social enterprise that seeks to facilitate productive entrepreneurial relationships between and among individuals within Africa and with the rest of the world. It aims to realize this through developing quality human resources, providing insight, generating relevant knowledge, and initiating and coordinating interactions.Under Noble Thrust Africa is the Strategic Development of young people through various mentorship initiatives. The Enlightened Career and the Adilifu Program.

INTEGRATIVE FACILITATIVE LEADERSHIP

Integrative Facilitative Leadership is “the spearheading of the pursuit of a mission, through the integration of various dimensions and enabling of access to the required resources. Integrative Facilitative Leadership is the approach for all Noble Thrust Initiatives. The three core words are integration, facilitation, and Leadership. Integration is the blending of separate units or dimensions to achieve coherence for the effective and efficient realization of an intended purpose. Leadership is the taking of responsibility within defined limits of authority for the fulfillment of a mission.

Enlightened Career

Professional development program based on the Stewardship Model of Professional Development (SMPD). The model advances four key stages of professional development, each consisting of specific blocks.

All these is within Noble Thrust’s Stewardship of Life philosophy of holistic personal development. These stages are Career Preparation, Career Foundation, Career Explore and Career Ultimate.

2

Hifadhi- Stawisha- College students’ empowerment Project

An enlightened career related initiative aimed at cultivating, protecting, and developing young college persons in order to realize and maintain focus and achieve a clear sense of purposeful pursuit in their life and career development.

It began during the Covid 19 pandemic to stabilize the students in preparation for the resumption of their studies. . It continues to relevantly prepare students on professional/field specific requirements for effective transition into the world of work.

3

Adilifu Program

A program focusing on development of expressive talents (music and other artistic talents)

The program consists of well-designed and individually customized musical expression, visual expression, non-musical vocal expression, and written expression developmental sessions, combined with biblical based reflections on self-discovery, effective personal development, and productive living.

4

Mentorship Facilitators Program

Identification and engagement of mentors as the core strategy in realizing the mentorship objectives.

The concept of mentorship facilitators is developed based on Noble Thrust’s Integrative Facilitative Leadership (IFL) approach.Mentorship facilitators are involved in facilitation within specific dimensions of the lives of mentees.
They play the connector role that enables access to knowledge, capacity and opportunities while seeking to maintain the big picture that ensures integration with other life dimensions of the mentees. They may not be the ones directly mentoring the individuals, but they enable access to mentors and mentorship opportunities and resources.
